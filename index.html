Prosthetics

In short, prostheses are artificial devices that replace missing body parts. This technology can help people, who, due to trauma, or disease, gain the ability to perform sports again. Due to advances in artificial limbs can be made with several types of stronger material, such as: plastic, carbon fiber. One person has even made his own personal prosthesis out of Lego. And newer developments may lead to artificial limbs connected to our brains, or robotically controlled, a common gadget in the future.

Xi'an International Football Centre

The Xi'an International Football Centre is a football stadium, which is currently under construction, in the Chinese city of Xi'an. Designed by Zaha Hadid Architects, this new stadium was set to be completed before the 2023 APC Asian cup, which unfortunately was moved to Qatar. Despite the circumstances, it is still a marvel of engineering, and while it won't be used by the time of the Asian cup, it will have cultural activities taking place when not being used for sports.

Abrasions

Abrasions during sport usually happens in the form of stumbling, sliding, or coming in contact with rough surfaces, and bacteria and other diseases can seriously harm your body.
